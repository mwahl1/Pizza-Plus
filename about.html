Our Story

We opened our doors in 1985 with a simple goal: serve pizza we'd be proud to eat every day. The recipe hasn't changed. Hand-stretched dough, slow-simmered sauces, real mozzarella, and serving our community for years to come!

Today, we're still that same neighborhood spot — just with a few more ovens and a lot more friends. Whether it's game night, a family dinner, or a quick lunch, we've got you covered.
